Social Media Analytics Tools

AI‑Driven Social Media Analytics Tools Cheat Sheet

This cheat sheet highlights analytics‑focused platforms that help tourism and food businesses collect data, benchmark performance and make strategic decisions. It prioritises tools that monitor existing social media pages rather than generate new content.

Tool Key Analytics & Insights Features Limitations Starting Price* Best For
Rival IQ1 Custom dashboards, engagement & follower metrics; alerts when competitors' posts trend; free head‑to‑head reports. No content creation; higher cost for more data history. $239/mo Tour operators/restaurants seeking deep competitor benchmarking.
Brandwatch Benchmark2 AI summarises content themes; share of voice, sentiment & hashtag metrics; recommends what/when to post. Enterprise pricing; complex interface. Enterprise (~$1k+/mo) Medium‑to‑large tourism brands needing strategic intelligence.
Mentionlytics3 Social listening & sentiment; SIA advisor recommends best times & hashtags; competitor and influencer monitoring. Price scales with mentions; no content generation. $49/mo (Basic) Businesses needing affordable listening & posting advice.
Meltwater4 Unlimited searches; AI trend & sentiment analysis; crisis detection; image recognition. High cost; overkill for small teams. ≈$10k+/yr Large tourism agencies requiring comprehensive listening.
Socialinsider5 AI content pillar insights; AI‑driven competitor analysis; post tagging for campaign tracking. Advanced features only in higher tiers; pricing starts at $99/mo. $99/mo Marketers & agencies analysing themes across networks.
SocialBee6 AI scheduling recommendations; category‑based scheduling; analytics up to 2 years. Limited competitor analysis; advanced analytics require higher tiers. $29–$99/mo Tourism businesses wanting all‑in‑one management & analytics.
Sprout Social (AI Assist)7 Premium analytics and listening; competitor reports; AI‑summarised insights. Expensive; targeted at mid‑size/enterprise clients. $199/user/mo Medium–large organisations needing robust analytics and management.
Hootsuite8 Side‑by‑side feed analytics; unified dashboard; compares up to two competitor pages. High cost; competitor comparison limited to two pages. $149/user/mo Agencies needing analytics plus scheduling & AI copywriting.
Predis.ai9 Basic analytics dashboard & goal‑tracking; competitor analysis of Facebook and Instagram. Limited to basic metrics; primarily a content generator. $27/mo (Lite) Small businesses wanting basic insights alongside AI generation.
ContentStudio10 Trending topics & sentiment metrics; influencer discovery; campaign performance grouping. Complex interface; AI quotas limited per plan. $19/mo Agencies monitoring trends across multiple topics.
Buffer (Analyze)11 Basic post analytics and reports; cross‑channel scheduling. No competitor insights; limited to simple metrics. $5 per channel/mo Creators needing affordable scheduling with simple analytics.

*Prices are approximate and refer to entry‑level paid plans (billed annually where noted). Always check the vendor's site for the latest pricing and features.
